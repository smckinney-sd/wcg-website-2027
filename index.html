The Wealth Consulting Group was built by advisors who believed the financial services industry could do better, for clients and for the advisors who serve them. From our origins in Las Vegas, we've grown into a nationwide network of planning-first advisors united by a common mission: to make life better for the clients they serve.

What We Stand For

Our Values

These aren't aspirations. They're the operating principles that guide every decision we make, for clients, for advisors, and for the firm.

Celebrate

We recognize progress, not just outcomes.

We celebrate advisor milestones, team wins, and personal achievements, because building something meaningful takes time. Growth isn't only measured in assets; it's measured in confidence, clarity, and momentum. We make space to acknowledge the progress, not just the outcome.

Why it matters: Advisors who feel seen stay engaged, motivated, and connected to the enterprise.

Excellence

We hold a high bar, and help each other reach it.

Excellence at WCG is intentional, disciplined, and practical. From planning and investments to service and operations, we commit to doing the work the right way, every time. We don't chase trends, we build systems that stand up over time.

Why it matters: Advisors can deliver consistently exceptional client experiences without reinventing the wheel.

Integrity

We do what's right, even when it's inconvenient.

Integrity guides our decisions with advisors, clients, partners, and each other. We value transparency over spin, alignment over short-term gain, and trust over transactions. When tradeoffs arise, we choose the long view.

Why it matters: Independence only works when trust is non-negotiable.

Humble

We lead with confidence, not ego.

We believe the best ideas can come from anywhere. Humility keeps us open, to feedback, to learning, and to continuous improvement. We listen first, act thoughtfully, and let results speak louder than rhetoric.

Why it matters: Advisors want partners who collaborate with them, not talk at them.

Diverse

We are stronger because we are different.

WCG values diversity of thought, background, experience, and perspective. There is no single "right" advisor model, growth path, or definition of success. We embrace individuality while building shared standards that elevate the whole community.

Why it matters: Better decisions, and better client outcomes, come from broader perspectives.

Collaboration

We build together, by design.

Collaboration at WCG is not optional or symbolic; it's structural. Advisors, leadership, and centralized teams work as one enterprise, sharing insight, resources, and accountability. We believe independence is strengthened, not diluted, by partnership.

Why it matters: Advisors don't want to be alone, but they don't want to give up control either.

Our Investment Philosophy

A Strategy Rooted in Research and Perspective.

We follow a core-satellite approach to asset management, designed to balance long-term discipline with the flexibility to adapt.

Our core portfolios are built using Modern Portfolio Theory (MPT), a method used by institutional investors to manage risk through strategic asset allocation.

Surrounding that foundation, our satellite positions reflect the guidance of our Investment Strategy Committee (ISC). The ISC actively monitors markets, identifies emerging trends, and adjusts positioning so your portfolio stays ahead, not behind.

◆

Core Portfolios

Built on Modern Portfolio Theory, the same institutional framework used by the world's largest endowments and pension funds, to manage risk through strategic asset allocation.

◆

Satellite Positioning

ISC-guided tactical positions that reflect current market intelligence, emerging trends, and forward-looking strategy, keeping your portfolio responsive to changing conditions.

◆

Broad Asset Universe

Model portfolios, ETFs, mutual funds, individual stocks, annuities, and alternative investments, blended and personalized to your plan.*

Our Process

Disciplined. Personalized. Built to Evolve.

At WCG, your Personal CFO follows a consistent, research-driven process designed to align your portfolio with your goals, adapting to life and market changes along the way.

Step 01

We begin by understanding your comfort with risk, your time horizon, and what you want your investments to help you achieve.

Step 02

Create Your Personal Investment Strategy

Using this input, we design a strategy that reflects your unique needs, values, and long-term vision.

Step 03

Diversify Across Asset Classes

We build your portfolio using a blend of asset classes and investment styles designed to manage risk and optimize performance.

Step 04

Rebalance as Conditions Change

Markets shift and life evolves. We rebalance your portfolio on an ongoing basis to keep it aligned with your strategy and objectives.

Step 05

Report and Review Regularly

You'll receive transparent account statements, custom performance reports, and dedicated review meetings, so you always know where you stand.

WCG Model Portfolios

Institutional Strategy, Personalized for Your Life.

We offer a range of asset-allocated model portfolios built around each client's goals, timeline, and risk profile. These portfolios are designed and maintained by our Investment Strategy Committee (ISC) and customized in collaboration with your personal advisor to reflect your specific needs.

Fee-Based, Client-First: Our model portfolios operate under a fee-based structure, not commissions. This ensures our incentives are aligned with your success and that every investment decision is made with your best interests in mind. Many portfolios are available through wrap accounts, which bundle advisory and trading costs into one transparent fee.

USA Today Best Financial Advisory Firm — 2026
Award based on client and peer recommendations and the short- and long-term growth of assets under management. USA Today Best Financial Advisory Firm — 2025 & 2023
This award was based on recommendations gathered through an independent survey of more than 25,000 participants (2025 / 2026) or more than 20,000 participants (2023), including clients, industry professionals, and financial advisors employed by other registered investment advisor (RIA) firms not affiliated with the award recipients. Firms were eligible for recognition if they met the following criteria: U.S.-based SEC-registered RIAs with more than $500 million in assets under management (AUM), a clean disciplinary record, and the provision of financial planning services and/or portfolio management for individuals and/or small businesses. Firms were selected based on an overall score placing them among the top 500 recipients. The selection of the firms and evaluation criteria were conducted by USA TODAY and Statista. Firms do not pay to be considered for the award; however, the firm paid a licensing fee to USA Today for the use of the award logo, which creates a financial benefit for the award sponsor. This award is not an endorsement and does not reflect client experience, number of clients served, or past or future investment performance. It is unrelated to any securities services separately offered by certain dually registered WCG representatives through unaffiliated broker-dealers.
Forbes Best-In-State Wealth Advisors — 2026
The Forbes Best-In-State Wealth Advisor ranking, developed by SHOOK Research, is based on in-person and telephone due diligence meetings and a ranking algorithm that includes: client retention, industry experience, review of compliance records, firm nominations; and quantitative criteria, including: assets under management and revenue generated for their firms. Portfolio performance is not a criterion due to varying client objectives and lack of audited data. Neither Forbes nor SHOOK Research receives a fee in exchange for rankings.
Forbes Best-In-State Wealth Advisors — 2024
The Forbes Best-In-State Wealth Advisor ranking announced in April 2024, developed by SHOOK Research, is based on in-person and telephone due diligence meetings and a ranking algorithm that includes: client retention, industry experience, review of compliance records, firm nominations; and quantitative criteria, including: assets under management and revenue generated for their firms. Key criteria include minimum 7 years of experience, over 50% of revenue must be generated from individual clients (not institutional) and a clean compliance history. Portfolio performance is not a criterion due to varying client objectives and lack of audited data. Firms do not pay to be considered for the award; however, the firm paid a licensing fee to Forbes/SHOOK Research for the use of the award logo, which creates a financial benefit for the award sponsor. This award is not an endorsement and does not reflect client experience, number of clients served, or past or future investment performance. It is unrelated to any securities services separately offered by certain dually registered WCG representatives through unaffiliated broker-dealers.
InvestmentNews Best Places to Work — 2026
The Wealth Consulting Group was named one of the "2026 InvestmentNews Best Places to Work" based on anonymous employee survey feedback. The award was issued on January 22, 2026, and recognizes firms with strong workplace culture and employee satisfaction. Eligible firms were required to have at least 15 U.S.-based employees, operate as a Registered Investment Adviser, Independent broker dealer, or hybrid firm, and be in business for a minimum of one year. Award consideration was based on data covering a trailing 12-month period. No fee was required to be considered for the recognition; however, a one-time licensing fee for lifetime usage applies. This recognition is based solely on employee feedback and is not indicative of investment performance, client experience, or the quality of advisory services. It should not be construed as an endorsement of the firm.

Barron's Top 100 RIA Firms
Barron's "Top 100 RIA Firms" ranking is based upon quantitative and qualitative criteria including: regulatory records, client retention reports, assets managed, revenue generated, technology spending, number of clients, size and diversity of staff, placement of a succession plan, and more. Citywire 50 Growers — 2024
The award is issued by Citywire based on data analysis by FINTRX of independent Registered Investment Advisor firms ("RIAs") with at least $200 million in assets under management ("AUM") as of December 31, 2024. Citywire recognized 50 recipients among 146 firms considered using three metrics derived from 2023 and 2024 regulatory filings: (1) year-over-year AUM, (2) percentage AUM growth, and (3) employee headcount. These metrics are combined into a single "growth factor" used to identify the fastest-growing RIAs in each state. Firms do not pay to be considered for the award; however, the firm paid a licensing fee to Citywire for the use of the award logo, which creates a financial benefit for the award sponsor. This award is not an endorsement and does not reflect client experience, number of clients served, or past or future investment performance. It is unrelated to any securities services separately offered by certain dually registered WCG representatives through unaffiliated broker-dealers.
